Zuihou Mini

Beginner Mandarin card game

Zuihou Mini is a simplified, classroom-ready version of the Zuihou system designed for beginning Mandarin learners. It emphasizes high-frequency vocabulary, tone awareness, and repeated exposure through structured play.

Learning Outcomes

  • Recognition of common Mandarin vocabulary
  • Increased awareness of tones
  • Familiarity with simplified Chinese characters

Classroom Use

Zuihou Mini is suitable for whole-class activities, small-group play, and review sessions. Games typically require minimal setup and can be completed within a standard class period.
